Meet the Speakers

Day 1 Keynote

Mr Piyush Gupta

Chairman, Keppel Ltd · Chairman, SMU Board of Trustees · Former CEO & Director, DBS Group

Mr Piyush Gupta led DBS Group as CEO and Director from 2009 to 2025, during which DBS was repeatedly named Best Bank and Best Digital Bank in the world, and recognised by Harvard Business Review as one of the top ten transformative organisations of the 2010 – 2020 decade. He spent 27 years at Citigroup prior to DBS, latterly as CEO for South-East Asia, Australia and New Zealand. He is Chairman of Keppel Ltd, Chairman of the Board of Trustees of Singapore Management University, and Chairman of Mandai Wildlife Group, and serves as Chairman, India at Temasek. He was conferred the Public Service Star in 2020 and the Pravasi Bharatiya Samman Award in 2023.

Day 1 · 10:10 AM Building Human Advantage in the Era of Disruption & AI
Day 5 Keynote

Ms Selena Ling

Chief Economist & Global Head, OCBC Group Research, Global Markets, OCBC

Selena leads OCBC Group Research, the team responsible for macroeconomic insights, forecasts and trading recommendations spanning macroeconomics, foreign exchange, interest rates, corporate credit, equity and ESG. Under her leadership, OCBC's research capabilities have been highly valued by customers and consistently top-ranked in industry surveys. She is Vice President and council member of the Economic Society of Singapore, sits on the advisory boards of NTU's College of Humanities, Arts and Social Sciences and the S. Rajaratnam School of International Studies, and was conferred the IBF Fellow Award in Financial Markets in 2017.

Day 5 · Closing Keynote Global Trends
EQ Series · Days 2 & 4

Dr Granville D'Souza

Group CEO and Founder, EQ World

For 29 years, Dr Granville has helped employees and leaders work with their strengths and blind spots through coaching and facilitative workshops on team and individual behaviour. A long-standing advocate for Emotional Intelligence in the workplace, he draws on practical tools and therapies to help people navigate emotional and interpersonal challenges at work.
